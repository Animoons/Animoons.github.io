Memories of an Ojibwe Childhood 1919 Maude Kegg p. 48

mikinaakoonsag little snapping turtles


mewinzha long ago gabekanaansing at portage lake ingii-daamin we lived.  miish so then a'aw that ninoshenh my auntie bezhig one wa'aw this ninoshenh my auntie adawewigamigong to the store izhaadaa let's go ikido s/he says.  miish so then wii-wiiji'iweyaan that I wanted to go with her. niwii-wiijiwaa I wish to go with her adaawewigamigong to the store izhaad as she goes.  ziinzibaakwadoons little sugars iko well nimiinig s/he gives me a'aw that waandane waandane gii-inaa s/he was called a'aw that adawewinini tradesman.  


niwenda-odendam I was just determined wii-wiiji'iweyaan that I want to go along gaa-bi-izhi-wiijiiwag that I go along with her.  waasa far ingii-izhaamin we went ingoji somewhere go well naanaan five maagizhaa maybe gaye and ingodwaaswi six diba'igan measures, miles.  

miish so then eta just go well miinawaa again imaa there gezikwendamaan that I barely remember it imaa there eni-bimoseyaang as we walk along miikana road odaabaanikaa vehicle road.  mii so eta just eniginind it was only as big as a'aw that odaabaan vehicle.  inzhaashaaginizide I am barefoot gaye and basikawaanagwaa as I kick them ingiw those asiniinsag stones imaa there ani-bimoseyaan as I walk along.


baanimaa later go well imaa there gaa-bimooded it had been crawling mikinaakoons little snapping turtle enda-agaashiinyi it is just small.  mii so imaa there izhi-inaabiyaan as I in such a way look there indigo similar naa also gaa-wanoodewaad as they had been crawling mikinaakoonsag snapping turtles.  mii so go well bijiinag just after gii-baashkaawe'owaad they had hatched.


wewiib hurry ikido s/he says aabanaabamid as s/he turns to look at me jibwaa-onaagoshig before evening ji-dagoshinaang in order that we arrive iwidi over there endaayaang where we live.

gaawiin no indinaa I say to her niwii-naganaasiig I don't want to leave them behind ingiw those mikinaakoonsag little turtles.  dibi wonder ge-izhaawaagwen that they will go.

enda-nishkaadizi s/he is just mad.  wiiwegin wrap her/him imaa there gidoodaazhising in your skirt!  maamigin pick them up! zaaga'iganiing by the lake ga-ani-izhaamin we will go ikido s/he says.


mii so imaa there endazhitaayaan as I just am enthralled maamiginagwaa thay I pick them up baadii'id as s/he waits for me imaa there maamiginagwaa that I pick them up ingoji somewhere go well nisimidanana thirty niibowa many sa ! go well mikinaakoonsag little snapping turtles imaa there ingoodaazhenzhishing in my little skirt ezhi-wiiweginagwaa as I in such a way wrap them bimoseyaang as we walk along.


gomaapii after awhile dash then igo well azhigwa already geget certain zaaga'igan lake imaa there ayaamagad that it is.  mii so imaa there obagidin release her/him ikido s/he says mii so ingiw those mikinaakoonsag little snapping turtles.  mii so eni-bimoseyaang as we walk along away gaa-izhi-izhaayaang that we had in such a way been going imaa there jiigibiig along the shore miish so then  imaa there bebezhig one after another ezhi-bagidinagwaa that I in such a way let them go imaa there nibiikaang in the water.  


ingosaag I fear for them ji-gibwanaabaawewaad that they could drown.

gaawiin no ikido s/he says mii so imaa there endazhiikewaad that is where they live ingiw those mikinaakwag snapping turtles ikido s/he says.  miish so then ezhi-bagidinagwaa that in such a way I put them down imaa there bebezhiig one after another ganawaabamagwaa as I watched them maamaajiikwazhiwewaad as they paddled hard enda-wawiyadendaagoziwag they are just funny, cute.   


mii so eta just imaa there minik a certain amount gezikwendamaan that I barely remember.
