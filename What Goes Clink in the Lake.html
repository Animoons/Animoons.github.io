gegoo something gii-madwesing make a noise from a distance place zaaga'iganiing lake


gichi-mewinzha very long ago imaa there ingii-noondam I heard something zaaga'igan lake bezhig one iwidi over there amanj uncertain iidog it seems ezhi-anishinaabewinkaadeg as in such a way it is called in the language. biiwaabikokaaning shining mineral place Ironton iwidi over there awas other side dagon it is in a place i'iw that zaaga'igan lake.


bezhig one giiwenh so they say a'aw that akiwenzii elder man ezhi-maajaad that as in such a way s/he leaves naadid as that s/he searches for it mashkiki medicine.  miish so then giiwenh so they say iwidi over there jiigibiig beach, shoreline ezhi-namadabid as in such a way s/he sits giiwenh as the story goes ezhi-nagamod that in such a way s/he is singing manidoo-nagamonan spirit songs ingoji somewhere dash then niiwin four niiwin four nagamonan songs iniw those manidoo-nagamonan spirit songs.  mii so giiwenh so they say ezhi-maajijiwang it in such a way begins to flow i'iw that zaaga'igan lake.  miish so then giiwenh supposedly ingoding once ingoding once igo well mii so imaa there awiiya someone ezhi-mookiid as in such a way s/he is emerging.  onikaa s/he has arms miinawaa also oshtigwaani s/he has a head akina all sa ! go well ezhi-naagozid that s/he in a such way appears igo well anishinaabe human ezhi-naagozid as s/he in a such way appears.  miish so then giiwenh so they say ezhi-maajitaad that in such a way s/he is starting manashkikiwed as s/he is making medicine onikaaning from her/his arm magizhaa maybe gaye and oshtigwaanining her/his head akina all go well gegoo something miinawaa also go well imaa there okaakiganaaning chest miinawaa again okaadining leg place. akina all go well mii so ezhi-bakwe'ang that in such a way a piece is broken off giiwenh so they say mii so i'iw that wegodogwen whatever dinowa kind.


miish so then giiwenh as the story goes giizhiitaad that s/he completes it mii so ezhi-gaganoonaad that in such a way s/he tells the other mii so i'iw that minik many waa-manashkikiwaaninaan as s/he wants to or probably will make medicine from you odinaan s/he says to the other giiwenh so the story goes. mii so miinawaa also ezhi-maajii-nagamod as in such a way s/he starts singing.  mii so miinawaa again ezhi-maajijiwang that in such a way it begins to flow zaaga'igans little lake.  miish so then giiwenh so the story goes ezhi-gondaataad that in such a way s/he goes under a'aw that manidoo mysterious being. 


ingoding once dash then igo well giiwenh so they say apane always go well gichi-mookomanag americans imaa there nibaawaad as they are sleeping ezhi-noondamowaad that they hear it i'iw that medwesininig as they are heard making the sound of a stone hitting something.  Mayagi-wewesinining as it makes a strange echo on stone. miish so then igo well gaa-izhi-gotawmowaad that in such a way they had been afraid of it ingiw those gichi-mookomaanag americans.  gaawiin no imaa there gii-izhaasiiwag they did not go.


miish so then ingoding once ingii-nagadenimaanaan we made friends with the other(s) gichi-mookoman other american(s).
