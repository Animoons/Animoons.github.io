Memories of an Ojibwe Childhood 1991 Maude Kegg p.41

jiimaan canoe


mewinzha long ago agaawaa barely ingezikwendaan I barely remember it amanj uncertain iidog it seems gaa-iniginiwaanen as I had been such a height madaabiiyaan as I started down to the shore.  jiimaan canoe imaa there gii-ate it was in a certain place. miish so then gaa-izhi-booziyaan that I had in such a way gotten onto it. magizhaa maybe gaye also iwidi over there ishkwe-ayi'ii at the end of it gaa-inaandawewaanen that I had climbed into it.  gaawiin no imaa there ingikendanziin I don't barely remember it minik a certain amount.


imaa there inaabiyaan as I look igo just waasa far away azhigwa already ayaamagad that it is i'iw that jiimaan canoe noondawagwaa as I hear them ikwewag women niswi three maagizhaa maybe gaye also niiwin four nookomis my grandmother miinawaa again go just ninoshenh my auntie miinawaa also go just ninoshenyag my aunties niizh two biijiba'idiwaad as they together come running towards bi-mawiwaad as they came on this way crying aanind several biidaadagaaziiba'idiwaad as they together run this way into the water gaa-izhi-zegiziyaan as I had been afraid.


ingikendaan I realized wiin s/he igo well zegiziyaan as I am afraid. maagizhaa maybe gaye also gaa-kwaashkwaniwaanen I must have jumped. baanimaa later dash then igo well miinawaa also gikendamaan as I know it iwidi over there ganawaabamagwaa as I watch them ingiw those giigoonyag fish eniwek very much igo just naa also anooj various ezhi-naagoziwaad as they in such a way are visible.  agwadaashiinsag sunfish ingiw those.  aangodinong occasionally gaye also enda-besho very nearby  inganawaabamigoog they watch me. mii so eta just go well imaa there minik a certain amount gaa-gikendamaan as I had known it.  maagizhaa maybe gaye and gaa-agwaabiiginigoowaanen that I had been dragged from the water by the other(s).
