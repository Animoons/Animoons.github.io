Ojibwa Texts II 1919 Story 33. gagige-binesii forever bird p.302-307


mii so iwe that niin I gaa-izhiwebiziyaan that I had such things happen to me megwa while gii-abinojiiwiyaan when I was a child. apii at this time naano-biboonigiziyaan when I was five winters old mii so iw that apii moment gii-gagaanzomid he compelled me noosiban my past father zhi-makade'keyaan to in such a way blacken my face (in order to fast). gigizheb very early gaawiin no ningii-ashamigoozii I was not fed; makade black (charcoal) ningii-atoon I put it shkiizhigong on my eyes. ningoji somewhere biinakamigaang in an open part of the woods ningii-babaa-izhaa I went from place to place. apii at this time idash then wenaagoshigin as it was becoming evening ningii-giiwe I went home bakadeyaan as I was hungry; mii so dash then bangii a little nindashamigo I am fed by someone. mii so go well iw that moozhag often gaa-izhi-gagaanzomigoyaan someone had in such a way compelled me zhi-makade'keyaan that I in such a way blacken my eyes/face (in order to fast), gegapii finally ningii-gashkitoon I succeeded ningo-giizhig one day ningo-dibik one night gaye and gii-wiisinisiiwaan that I did not eat.


apii at such a moment idash then eni-mindidooyaan as away I become big naaniingodinoo from time to time, now and again ningii-niizhoganii I went two days gaawiin no biindig inside wiigiwaaming in the birch house ningii-nibaasii I slept not; ningoji somewhere biinakamigaang in a clearing ningii-awi-nibaa I went on and slept. gegapii finally anooj various gegoon something ningii-a-inaabandam I dreamed. apiji very much idash then geshkitooyaan that I am able niiyogan four days wiisinisiiwaan that I did not eat it gaawiin no eta just ajina a little while i'iw that ningii-izhichigesii I such a way did not do apane always go well moozhag often moozhag often ningii-gii'igwishim I sought a vision. gegapii finally ningii-waabamaag I saw them anishinaabeng like humans ezhinaagoziwaad that in such a way they appear. "noozhis, my grandchild, gibizhawenimin, I come to pity/bless you," ningii-igoog I was told by them. miziwe everywhere ningii-babaa-izhi-winigoog I from place to place in such a way was brought by them; gii-babaa-waabanda'igooyaan that I was here and there it shown miziwe everywhere aki earth ezhinaagwak that they appear so, gichigami great water gaye also. apii at this time idash then gaa-gashkitooyaan I had succeeded shwaasagan eight days gii-wiisinisiiwaan that I did not eat mii so iwe that apii moment geget yes miziwe everywhere gigikendamaan that I know it ezhinaagwak that in such a way they appear giizhigong in the sky. mii so sa very much i'iw that gaa-izhi-waabandamaan that I had in such a way seen it apiji very gichi-mistzhaamagad that it is big and great iwe that giizhig sky. gaawiin no ningoji somewhere wekweyaasinoon it is not angular gakina all anangoog stars ayaawaad as they are; ningii-waabanda'igoo I was shown by them gakina all ezhiwebak that in such a way happens anangoog stars ayaawaad as they are. niibiwa many bemaadizijig beings iwidi over there ayaawag there are anangong in the stars. mii so go well iw that gakina all anangoog stars ayaawaad that they are ezhiwebak that in such a way happens. gaye and dash then ishpiming in the above place eyaad that s/he is manidoo mystery, spirit ningii-waabamaa I saw her/him. nibaayaan as I sleep weweni careful aanawi though ningii-gaganoonik I was spoken to by them; mii so go well iw that gakina all giizhig the sky enigokwaak that it is the size of mii go iw that manidoo mystery, spirit wiiya someone, person. gaawiin no gakina all gidebabamaasiinaanig we cannot perceive them anangoog stars gaye and giizisoog suns; gaawiin no biijinikaasiiwag they do not move, oo'owe this dash but aki earth giijibaakaa moves.


aanawi anyway, besides mii so iw that gaa-izhi-waabandamaan that I had in such a way seen it gichi-miikanan (a) great road inamanoon it leads ezhaad as s/he goes giizis (the) sun, gaye and dibikigiizis moon; gakina all gaye also anangoog stars ezhaawaad as they go. gaye and anooj various gegoon something ningii-waabandaan that I saw it ishpiming in the above place eyaamagak as they are.


gaye and dash then gaye and niin I ningii-gikendan I knew it i'iw that jiisakiiwin shaking tent way. niswi three ayaawan there are iniw those jiisakaanan shake tents. gakina all waabandamowaad that they see anishinaabeg the people niibowa many gaye also niiyawing (are) in my body; ningii-danendaan I contemplated i'iw that gaa-bawaadamaan that I had dreamed of megwa while gii-oshkinawewiyaan I had been a youth. gakina all giizhaa beforehand ningii-waabandaan I saw it minik so much of gaa-bi-izhiwebiziyaan that I had come to experience baa-gobimaadiziyaan as place to place I lived. eniwek very much igo well waawaasa very far ningii-babaa-izhaa I had from place to place gone. gaawiin no ningoji anywhere ningii-mayaginasiin I was not unfamiliar; aki earth giizhaa beforehand gakina all ningii-waabandaan I saw it megwa while gii-oshkibimaadiziyaan I had been a new relative; oodenawaan towns gaye also. gakina all gaye and bemaadizijig beings gaawiikaa never awiya someone ningii-mayagenimaasii I was not a stranger. apiji very ningii-zaagi'igoog they were open to me bemaadizijig (the) beings, (the) people minik many gaa-bawaabamagwa that I had seen about; ikwewag women gaye also apiji very much niizaagi'igoog love me, apiji very much gaye also ningii-nitaa'andawenjige I was skilled at hunting. gaawiin no gegoon something ningii-agaawaadansiin I did not wish for in vain megwa while gii-gashkitooyaan I was able to gii-andawenjigeyaan that I hunted. gaye and dash then apii at that time ayaamagak as there is oo'o this anokiiwin work memindage to a great degree moozhag often ningii-anoonigoo I was hired zhi-babemaadiziyaan that I in such a way go about living. miziwe everywhere gegoo something gii-izhiwichigaade it was taken eyindagok where that they live awhile biboonizhiwinoon winter camps, eniwek considerably gaye also ningii-mashkawizii I was strong megwa while gii-ininiiwiyaan as I was a man. naanan five mashkimodan sacks ningii-gashkitoonan I was able to gii-bimoondamaan that I carried it on my back.


anishaa it is only that go well nindibaajim I tell of gaa-bi-izhiwebiziyaan what had happened to me. noongom now idash then gaawiin no geyaabi still i'iw that nind-izhi-mashkawi-bimaadizii I am not in such a way living with strength; agaawa barely noongom now ningashkitoon I am able wenji-bimaadidiyaan that I provide a living. anishaa in vain aanind several weyaabishkiiwejig those that are white mewinzha long ago gaa-gikenimiwaad as they had known me bebangii little by little gegoo something niminigoog they give me baasikamaan that I wear. aaniish how then mii so iw that.
