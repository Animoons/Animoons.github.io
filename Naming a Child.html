Naming a Child Ojibwa Texts II 1919 Story 41 p.331


dibaajimod as told by gaagige- forever binesi bird, thunderbird John Binesi


mii so iw that ezhichigewaad as/that they in such a way do oo'omaa here ingiw those anishinaabeg. people. giishpin if awiya someone waabamaad as s/he sees the other(s) abinoojiiyan child other(s) ingiw those dash, then, weniijaanisiwed as s/he has a child onandamaawaan they look for the other(s) bezhig one akiwenziyan, other elder man, gomaa or gaye and mindimooyeyan. other elder woman. ogagwejimaawaan: they ask the other(s): gaawiin no ina question gidaa-wiinaasii you could not name her/him a'aw that one niniijaanisinaan? our child? giishpin if dash, then, eyenh yes ikidod that s/he says a'aw that akiwenzi, elder man, giizhaaj beforehand bangi little bit gegoo something omiinaawaan, they give it to the other(s), memindage especially asemaan his/her tobacco. mii so dash then i'iw that one enaawaad that they tell the other(s) zhi-ozhitoowaad in such a way they will make it wiikwandiwin. feast. a'aw that one idash then akiwenzi elder man naanaagadawendam s/he contemplates (ga)apii moment enaabandang that s/he dreams of it megwa while gii-bi-oshki-bimaadizid as s/he was newly living gii-makade'ked when s/he fasted. giizhaaj beforehand onaanaagadawenimaan s/he contemplates the other(s) iniw other(s) abinoojiiyan other child waa-wiinaad that s/he will most likely name. apii moment idash then i'iw that one not in motion ojijiseg as it goes by giizhigad that it is day, iw that apii moment gii-waawiindamaawaad that they promised the other wii-wiigokewaad that they would make a feast, apii moment gaa-izhitoowaad as they in such a way had made it iw that one not in motion wiikwanidiwin feast, onandamaawaan they send for the other iniw that other(s) akiwenziiyan other elder man. gegoo something idash then omiinawaan s/he gives to the other agwaniiwin blanket, gaye and dash then i'iw that miijim food gaa-giizhideg that which had been cooked omiinawaan s/he gives to the other. wiin s/he idash then a'aw that one in motion akiwenzii elder man onandamaan s/he sends for the other aanind several anishinaaben other person(s), wiin s/he igo well, um, ah odoonenimaan s/he decides about the other minik many ge-nandamindwa they that will be asked anishinaabeg people. mii so dash then iw that one not in motion apii moment gaa-giigidod as/that s/he had spoken a'aw that one in motion akiwenzi elder man dazhindang that s/he talks about it i'iw that one not in motion gegoo something gaa-gikendang that s/he had known megwa while gii-oshkiniigid when s/he was young; owaa-wiindaan s/he names the other i'iw that inakake to such a direction waa-izhi-winaad as s/he wishes to take the other(s) to such a place iniw the other(s) abinoojiiyan the other child. gakina all gegoo something wayaabandang that s/he sees it anishinaabe person mii so imaa there wendinamowaad that they get something anishinaabeg people wiindaawasowaad that they name a child; gonimaa maybe owe this aki earth gomaa or gaye also gichigami sea, gomaa or gaye also baawitig rapids, gomaa or gaye also endaso-odaanimak every(where) that the wind blows, gomaa or gaye also giizisoog suns, anangoog stars, aanakwad cloud, mitigoog trees, asin stone, gonimaa maybe gaye also gakina all ingiw those awesiiyag animals gakina all ingiw those giigoonyag fish, gomaa or gaye also gakina all bebaamisejig those that from place to place fly binesiwag birds. mii so imaa there gakina all wendinamowaad that they get something waandawasowaad that they name a child.



naanigodinoo once in awhile zhiingwewidamwag in such a way they make noise speaking anishinaabeg people wiindawasowaad as they name a child. naaniingodinoo once in awhile gaye also nagamo s/he sings a'aw that anishinaabe person wiindawaasod s/he that gives the name; mii so dash then imaa there megwa while nagamod as s/he sings ani-wiindang as away s/he names the other i'iw that izhinikaazowin name ezhinikaanaad that s/he calls the other iniw other(s) abinoojiyan other child.mii so iw that gaa-onji-izhichigewaad this is why they had in such a way done, naaniingodinoo once in a while awiya someone gii-onji-bimaadizi s/he lived because giishpin if gii-wiinzod it is that s/he was named abinoojii child. gakina all dash then wii-gidaan'aawa they wish to eat it all i'iw that miijim food gaa-giizhidenig it that had been cooked. mii so sa ! gaa-izhichigewaad as they had done ingiw those anishinaabeg people wandaawaziwaashin as they want to name it. ingiw those dash then anishinaabeg people gaa-wiinzonid when the other had been named oniijaanisiwaan the other child weniijaanisingin the people who name the child ogandiinenamaawaan they understand to be the parent iniw the other gaa-miinaawaad that had given to the other onijaanisiwaan other child. naaniingodinoo once in awhile miinawaa also oga-wiikwange'aawaan they will make a feast for the other iniw that oniijaanisiwaan other child bagosendamowaad as they hope ginwenj for a long while zhi-bimaadizinid that in such a way the other lives.
