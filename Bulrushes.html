gichimgamiiwashkoon bulrushes


miinawaa again mewinzha long ago gichigamiiwashkoon bullrushes mamood that s/he gathers zaaga'iganiing at the lake miish so then iniw. those. ode'imini-giizis strawberry moon gii-ikido s/he says ko well jibwaa-aabita-niibing. before middle summer. mii so asemaan tobacco ezhi-bagidinaad as in such a way s/he offers it azhigwa already wii-mamood that s/he wishes to gather it iniw those ayi'iin they are gichigamiiwashkoon. bullrushes. gaawiin no giiwenh so it is said awiiya somebody odaa-izhi-mamoosiinan s/he should take them not gii-ikido she said ko. well. apane always ingii-naanaagadawaabamaa I watched her carefully gaa-izhichiged. that s/he had done. miish then i'iw that jiimaaning-sh in the canoe then boozi she gets in wiigwaasi-jiimaaning in the birch bark canoe gaye. also. mii so ezhi-bakwajibidood as s/he in such a way breaks a piece off mii so iniw those gichigamiiwashkoon bullrushes ikidowag they say izhinikaadenig. as in such a way they are called. mii so azhigwa already niibowa a lot ezhi-bakwajibidood as/that s/he in such a way breaks a piece off mii so ezhi-ayaad in such a way as s/he is ezhi-agwaasidood that in such a way s/he brings it to shore mii so ezhi-maawandoopidood. as s/he in such a way ties them together. mii so ezhi-giishkizhang that s/he cuts them iwidi over there ojiibikaawaninig. at the roots. mii so dash then imaa there namadabiyaan as I sit iko well maamiijiyaan as I eat it mii so iniw those. ingii-kina'amaag-sh s/he taught me then wiin. s/he. gego don't miijiken eat it niibowa many ingii-ig. s/he told me. enda-minopogwadoon they just taste good ojiibikaawang roots iniw those gichigamiiwashkoon bullrushes.
